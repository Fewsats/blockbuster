Frequently Asked Questions

What is Blockbuster?

Blockbuster is a video sharing platform that allows users to upload, share, and monetize their videos using Lightning Network payments.

How do I sign up for Blockbuster?

You can sign up by clicking the "Sign In / Sign Up" button and entering your email address. A magic link will be sent to your email for authentication.

How do I upload a video?

You can upload a video by filling out the upload form on the main page, including title, description, price, cover image, and video file.

How do view my uploaded videos?

Uploaded videos are associated to an email account. To see the list of your videos you have to sign in using the same email you provided during the videos upload.

How do I set a price for my video?

When uploading a video, you can set the price in cents in the "Price (in cents)" field of the upload form.

What payment method does Blockbuster use?

Blockbuster uses the L402 protocol & the Lightning Network for payments, which allows for fast and low-cost payments.

What is an L402 URI?

An L402 URI is a special link that includes payment information for accessing the video. It's used to facilitate the Lightning Network payment process and they look like this:


l402://blockbuster.fewsats.com/video/info/72142

Can I edit my uploaded videos?

Currently, video editing is not supported. You can only upload and manage your videos.

How do I contact support?

For support inquiries, please use the Intercom support button on the bottom right to contact us or send an email to admin@fewsats.com.
